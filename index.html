Welcome to Trinity Boxing Club

Leeds Night Owls is a friendly, long established tabletop wargaming club that meets in the heart of Leeds. In 2024, the club celebrated its 25th anniversary.

Get in Touch

The Night Owls Difference

Just Bring Miniatures

Night Owls has a wide range of scenery from sci-fi, historical and fantasy in a range of scales from 6mm all the way up to 28mm.

Everyone Gets a Game

Whatever happens you are always guaranteed a game whether you've organised a game or not. At every meeting there is always at least one table anybody can play at.

Play What you Want

You can play whatever games you want. Night Owls doesn't specify what games you can play.

Free Parking

The Cardigan Centre has on-site parking you may use for free.

Tuck Shop

Night Owls run a tuck shop during meetings. You can buy tea & coffee and soft drinks as well as chocolate bars and biscuits at modest cost.

Easy to Reach by Public Transport

Burley Park railway station is a short walk away. Cardigan Road is also served by a number of buses as is Kirkstall Road a short walk away.

Frequently Asked Questions

The club meets every Sunday between 10am and 2pm.

The club meets at The Cardigan Centre on Cardigan Road in Leeds. For more details about where we meet, please see the meeting page.
